## Dimension Details Template

For each dimension with notable findings:

```markdown
### {Dimension Name} ({score}/100)

**Strengths:**
- {positive finding}

**Weaknesses:**
- {issue or concern}

**Recommendations:**
1. {actionable improvement}
```

## Recommendations Summary

Include three sections:
- **Immediate Actions**: Critical/security fixes
- **Short-term Improvements**: High-priority items
- **Long-term Goals**: Quality targets
